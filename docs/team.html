Meet the Development Team!

Jana Amin

Jana is Robin's project manager and full-stack engineer. She is developed the bird detection algorithm, integrating voice commands for hands-free navigation, and collaborating with our clients to facilitate team operations.

Meriam Harissa

Meriam is the lead researcher and full-stack engineer. She created a new machine learning algorithm that improves bird prediction accuracy and optimized this model to detect the best bird sighting location for a user based on their current location.

Jodi Joven

Jodi is the lead UI/UX designer and full-stack engineer. She designed the entire user interface with an original design system and enabled the app's compatibility with screen readers on both iOS and Android devices for users who utilize assistive technologies.

Leah Mirch

Leah is Robin's scrum master and full-stack engineer. She developed the original Robin chatbot, which is an interactive AI chatbot powered by OpenAI that provides bird information, while also organizing and maintaining the project's backlog.
