What is Robin?

Robin leverages cutting-edge AI and environmental data to help you connect with nature. Whether you’re in your backyard or on the trail, Robin listens for bird calls and identifies species instantly—no binoculars required.

  • Real-time bird song recognition using your microphone.
  • Localized bird forecasts based on your location.
  • Voice guidance & accessibility for users of all abilities.
  • Interactive species chat for Q&A about your favorite birds.

By analyzing acoustic patterns and location data, Robin suggests likely bird sightings and helps you learn fascinating facts about local species. Join us in exploring the world of birds—right from your phone!
