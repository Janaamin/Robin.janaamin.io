What is Robin?

Robin is an AI-powered bird observation app that transforms the way you experience nature. Using real-time bird sound recognition, voice control, and location-based insights, Robin helps you identify birds around you and learn more about your local ecosystem.

Whether you're a hobbyist, educator, or birding by ear, Robin is built to make birdwatching inclusive, accessible, and fun.
