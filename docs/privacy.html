Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Robin, the Intelligent Bird Observation App (“we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when using our application.

1. Information We Collect

Microphone: We request access to your device's microphone to capture ambient bird sounds for real-time identification purposes. The audio data is processed in real time and is not stored or shared.

Location: We request access to your location to provide accurate, localized birdwatching forecasts and mapping of bird sightings. This information is used exclusively to enhance your user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Bird Identification: To analyze bird sounds captured by your microphone and return accurate species identifications.
  • Local Insights: To generate personalized birdwatching predictions based on your current location.
  • Usage Analytics: To help us understand app performance and improve functionality.

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Audio and location information is processed in real time and never stored on our servers.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and become effective immediately upon posting.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@example.com.
