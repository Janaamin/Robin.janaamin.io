Get Started in 5 Easy Steps

  1. Download Robin from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. On launch, grant microphone and location permissions.
  3. Tap the microphone icon to start bird sound recognition.
  4. View identified species and local bird forecasts.
  5. Check the log for your birding history.
